📊 Methodology

Energy Measurement

GPU power draw is sampled via nvidia-smi during inference. Energy (Wh) is computed by trapezoidal integration of the power-time curve. Historical messages use a token-based estimate (0.9 mWh/token, calibrated from Tesla P40 measurements).

Ecolyxis CO₂e

Cloud Baseline

US data-centre average: {{ cloud_grid_factor }} kg CO₂e/kWh (IEA 2024). PUE: {{ cloud_pue }} (Uptime Institute 2024 survey). This is the emissions a typical cloud AI provider would generate for the same compute.

Equivalents

CO₂e equivalents are based on UK government conversion factors: 1 smartphone charge ≈ 0.4g CO₂e, 1 km driven ≈ 170g CO₂e, 1 tree absorbs ≈ 21kg CO₂e/year.
