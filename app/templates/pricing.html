FAQ

What counts as a message?

Each message you send and each AI response counts as one message. On the free tier, you can send up to 5 messages per hour. Premium has no limits.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — cancel your subscription at any time from the billing page. You'll keep Premium access until the end of your current billing period.

What is the intro price?

Your first month of Premium is £4.99, then £9.99/month after that. No commitment, cancel before the first renewal and you'll only pay £4.99.

How does API billing work?

Add credits to your wallet via Stripe. API calls are deducted per token at £2.78 per million tokens. Top up anytime from the dashboard.
